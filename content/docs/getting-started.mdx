### Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:
- A valid email address or social media account (e.g., Twitter, Discord) for sign-up
- A crypto wallet (e.g., MetaMask) for connecting to the platform (optional for full features)
- A stable internet connection

### Step-by-Step Instructions

#### Step 1: Sign Up for PiP World

1. Visit the [Sign Up page](/docs/onboarding/sign-up) and choose your preferred method:
   - **Email**: Enter your email and create a password.
   - **Social**: Connect via Twitter or Discord for quick onboarding.
   - **Wallet**: Link a crypto wallet for seamless transactions (e.g., pack purchases).
2. Check your email for a verification link (see [Email Confirmation](/docs/onboarding/email-confirmation)).
3. Confirm your email to unlock full access to the platform.

<Callout type="warn">
  Verify your email within 24 hours, or your account may be temporarily locked!
</Callout>

#### Step 2: Set Up Your Profile

1. Go to [Profile Setup](/docs/onboarding/profile-setup).
2. Choose a username and avatar to personalize your account.
3. Optionally connect a wallet to enable pack minting and agent interactions.
4. Set notification preferences (e.g., for trading run updates or XP gains).

<Callout type="info">
  A custom avatar makes you stand out on leaderboards‚Äîpick something bold!
</Callout>

#### Step 3: Back Your First Agent

1. Navigate to [Agents Overview](/docs/agents/overview) or [My Agents](/docs/agents/my-agents).
2. Browse available Market Mavericks (influencer-led AI traders).
3. Review agent stats like win rate, ROI, and rarity (Common, Rare, Legendary).
4. Select an agent to back with virtual funds (see [Choosing Your First Agent](/docs/onboarding/first-agent)).

<Callout type="note">
  Start with a Common or Rare agent to learn the system before chasing Legendary ones.
</Callout>

#### Step 4: Explore Packs and XP

1. Visit [Packs Overview](/docs/packs/overview) to browse collectible packs.
2. Purchase a starter pack to unlock agents, XP boosts, or other rewards.
3. Open your pack to reveal contents (see [Pack Opening](/docs/packs/pack-opening)).
4. Complete your first XP task, like ‚ÄúLink Wallet: 55 XP,‚Äù via [XP Tasks](/docs/progression/xp-tasks).

#### Step 5: Check Leaderboards and Progress

1. Head to [XP Leaderboard](/docs/leaderboards/xp-leaderboard) to see your rank (highlighted in purple).
2. Explore [PnL Leaderboard](/docs/leaderboards/pnl-leaderboard) to track your agent‚Äôs performance.
3. Join a weekly trading run on [Trading Runs](/docs/leaderboards/trading-runs) to compete for rewards.

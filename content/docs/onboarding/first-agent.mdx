## Choosing Your First Agent

In **PiP World Market Mavericks**, you start by selecting **three influencer-led Market Mavericks** to stake your $10,000 virtual funds. This unlocks the [Trading Floor](/docs/agents/trading-floor), earns you **50 XP**, and awards the **First Team Badge**.

### How It Works

1. **Browse Agents**: Visit [My Agents](/docs/agents/my-agents) or [Agents Overview](/docs/agents/overview) to see available Mavericks (Common, Rare, Legendary).
2. **Review Stats**: Check each agentâ€™s win rate, ROI, and trading style (e.g., aggressive, balanced).
3. **Stake Funds**: Assign part of your $10,000 to three agents via the [Trading Floor](/docs/agents/trading-floor).
4. **Earn Rewards**: Get 50 XP and the First Team Badge upon confirming your selections.

### Key Points
- You must pick exactly three agents to unlock trading.
- Check [Agent Detail](/docs/agents/agent-detail) for performance stats before staking.
- Rewards appear in [Activity Feed](/docs/dashboard/activity-feed) and [Badges](/docs/progression/badges).

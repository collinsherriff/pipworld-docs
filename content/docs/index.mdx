## Introduction

Welcome to **PiP World Market Mavericks**—the ultimate virtual trading platform where you back influencer-led Market Mavericks, collect exclusive packs, and dominate global leaderboards!

<Callout type="info">
  Whether you're new to virtual trading or a seasoned player, PiP World offers thrilling gameplay, strategic depth, and rewarding progression for everyone.
</Callout>

### What is PiP World Market Mavericks?

PiP World Market Mavericks blends financial strategy with gamified fun. Build your portfolio, earn XP, and compete to become a trading legend. Key features include:

- 📈 **Agent Trading** - Back influencer-led AI agents to grow your virtual portfolio.
- 🎴 **Collectible Packs** - Buy and open packs with rare agents, XP boosts, and more.
- 🏆 **Leaderboards** - Compete globally in Profit & Loss (PnL), XP, and weekly trading runs.
- 🧠 **Progression System** - Earn XP through tasks and referrals to level up.
- 🛡️ **Badges & Rewards** - Unlock achievements like Trading Run Champion.

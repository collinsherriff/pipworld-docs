## What are Levels?

**Levels** in PiP World Market Mavericks track your progression based on experience points (XP). Higher levels unlock new agents, packs, badges, and potential future rewards like airdrops.

<Callout type="info">
  Level up to access exclusive features and show off your progress!
</Callout>


#### Flow
1. Earn XP through tasks and referrals ([XP Tasks](/docs/progression/xp-tasks)).
2. Track progress on the [Dashboard](/docs/dashboard-overview) Level Progress Bar.
3. Unlock rewards as you hit level thresholds.

<Callout type="warn">
  Don’t miss daily XP tasks—small gains add up to big level jumps!
</Callout>

### Key Points
- Levels require increasing XP thresholds.
- Unlocks include better agents and badges.
- Progress is permanent and boosts [XP Leaderboard](/docs/leaderboards/xp-leaderboard) rank.
